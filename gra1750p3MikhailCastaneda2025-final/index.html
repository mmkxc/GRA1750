Tiramisu

A simple, straightforward recipe for tiramisu. This classic, no-bake Italian dessert requires just 5 ingredients and a few hours in the fridge. Experiment with different flavors to make this recipe your own, or try it as is for the perfect morning treat, post-dinner dessert, or afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mascarpone, room temperature
  • ~24 ladyfingers
  • 1 cup espresso, room temperature
  • 4 eggs at room temperature, separated
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Whisk egg whites with 3 tablespoons of white sugar to form a soft meringue; do not overmix. Beat until soft peaks form, and set aside.
  2. Add remaining 3 tablespoons of sugar to egg yolks and combine with mascarpone. Whisk until smooth; the resulting combination should be pale and thick.
  3. Carefully fold meringue into egg yolk mixture, adding one third at a time until entirely combined.
  4. Dip ladyfingers briefly into espresso to cover both sides. Arrange dipped ladyfingers into a single, flat layer at the bottom of dish.
  5. Cover layer of dipped ladyfingers with half of the egg-mascarpone cream. Top with another layer of dipped ladyfingers, and then cover second layer of ladyfingers with the remaining cream.
  6. Chill tiramisu in the fridge for 6-8 hours. Then: enjoy!
